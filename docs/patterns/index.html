In Progress demo page

Step 1

Visit the Excelsior Pass web portal at epass.ny.gov

Step 2

Answer a few questions to confirm your vaccine or test information to retrieve your Pass

Step 3

Pass retrieved! Print your Pass, take a screenshot, or download the free Wallet App to store your Pass on your phone

Step 4

Present your Pass at participating organizations to verify your proof of vaccination or negative test result

Excelsior Pass is a voluntary platform. Your data and information is kept secure at all times.





1. Excelsior Pass empowers New Yorkers to easily access and maintain their personal health information and own proof of COVID-19 immunization or testing records.

2. Using secure technology, Excelsior Pass generates a unique, scannable Pass QR code that contains no personal health information in it. It is only used to verify an individualâ€™s vaccination or negative test result status.

3. The Pass QR code is generated and made available to the user based on vaccination and testing information that already exists in the secure New York State and New York City immunization and testing registries.

4. Passes contain cryptographic signatures that ensure each Pass is genuine and that no data-tampering has occurred.

5. While Excelsior Pass reduces the need to deal with formal test results and vaccination records like a CDC Card, users choose if, how, or when to present their Pass, which can be stored on the free Excelsior Pass Wallet app, or printed from the Excelsior Pass website portal for those who may not own smartphone devices. Individuals can always present alternate forms of vaccination or negative test results if they prefer.
